Introduction to FY1 Teaching Series

Hosted by Dr Kira Mattison, join us every Tuesday for a practical, interactive and high‑yield series designed for incoming foundation doctors to prepare for life on the wards—from ward rounds to portfolios and everything in between.

Join us weekly and feel more confident on the wards! This is not a one‑off class.

Date: Every Tuesday starting from 3 June
Time: 7:00 pm
Location: Online – MS Teams
